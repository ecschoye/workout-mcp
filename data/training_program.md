# Training Program

## Workout A: Push, Quads & Side Delts
~1h 14min, 27 sets

1. Leg Press (Machine) — 3x8-12, rest 2:30
   - Feet high on platform for glutes. Do NOT let hips lift off seat at bottom.
2. Bench Press (Dumbbell) — 3x8-12, rest 2:30
   - Bench supports spine. Keep feet flat or on bench to prevent arching.
3. Chest Supported Incline Row (Dumbbell) — 3x10-12, rest 2:00
   - Chest must stay glued to pad. Isolates back without lumbar load.
4. Leg Extension (Machine) — 3x12-15, rest 2:00
5. Lateral Raise (Dumbbell) — 3x12-15, rest 2:00
6. Triceps Pushdown — 3x12-15, rest 2:00
7. Bicep Curl (Dumbbell) — 3x10-12, rest 2:00
8. Sit Up (Weighted, Decline) — 3x12, rest 1:30
9. Back Extension (Weighted Hyperextension) — 3x12-15, rest 1:30

## Workout B: Pull, Hams & Arms
~1h 7min, 25 sets

1. Leg Press Horizontal (Machine) — 3x10, rest 2:30
2. Shoulder Press (Dumbbell) — 4x8-10, rest 2:00
   - Use high-incline bench (75-80 degrees), not vertical. Pressing slightly forward is safer than directly overhead.
3. Pull Up (Weighted) — 3x6-8, rest 2:00
   - Keep legs slightly in front (hollow body), do not cross ankles behind (promotes arching).
4. Seated Leg Curl (Machine) — 3x10-12, rest 2:00
5. Glute Bridge — 3x10-12, rest 1:00
   - Direct glute work corrects pelvic tilt. Safer than hip thrusts (less shear).
6. Hammer Curl (Dumbbell) — 3x10-12, rest 2:00
7. Seated Calf Raise — 3x15-20, rest 2:00
8. Hip Adduction (Machine) — 3x12-15, rest 2:00

## Rotation
Alternating A/B split. Typically 2-3 strength sessions per week.

## Cardio: Elliptical (12-Week Plan, started 2026-02-24)
Preferred cardio modality (no treadmill — flat feet). 3 sessions/week on non-lifting days or after lifting.

### HR Zones (age 25, est. max 195)
- Z2: 120-145 bpm (conversational, easy breathing)
- Z3: 146-163 bpm (comfortably hard)
- Z4: 164-180 bpm (hard, can't talk)
- Z5: 181-195 bpm (all-out)

### Phase 1: Aerobic Base (Weeks 1-4, Feb 24 - Mar 23)
All sessions Zone 2 (120-145 HR). Will feel easy — that's the point.
- Session 1: Z2 Steady, 25 min
- Session 2: Z2 Steady, 30 min
- Session 3: Z2 Long, 35-45 min (add 5 min/week)

### Phase 2: Introduce Tempo (Weeks 5-8, Mar 24 - Apr 20)
Two Z2 sessions + one Z3 tempo session.
- Session 1: Z2 Steady, 30 min
- Session 2: Z3 Tempo, 25 min (10 min Z2 warmup, then 15-20 min at Z3; add 2 min/week)
- Session 3: Z2 Long, 45-50 min

### Phase 3: Add Intervals (Weeks 9-12, Apr 21 - May 18)
Two Z2 sessions + one interval session.
- Session 1: Z2 Steady, 30 min
- Session 2: Intervals, 25-30 min (rotate weekly):
  - Option A: 10 min warmup, 6x (1 min Z4-5 / 2 min Z2), 5 min cooldown
  - Option B: 10 min warmup, 4x (2 min Z4 / 2 min Z2), 5 min cooldown
  - Option C: 10 min warmup, 8x (30s all-out / 90s easy), 5 min cooldown
- Session 3: Z2 Long, 50-55 min

### Rules
- 80/20 split: ~80% of cardio minutes in Z2, ~20% hard
- Watch HR, not pace/resistance — go slow enough to stay in zone
- Never skip easy sessions to "make up" with a hard one
- Do cardio on non-lifting days, or after lifting (never before)
- Don't add a 4th session until consistent at 3x/week for 4+ weeks

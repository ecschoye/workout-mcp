## Assessment Protocol
Before each workout:

2. **90/90 Hip Stretch** x 30s each (hip flexor tightness)
3. **Wall Test** (lumbar flattening ability)
4. **Pain scale** (1-10) for upper back, lower lumbar, general stiffness

## Progression Strategy
**Week 1-2:** Form focus, no weight
**Week 3-4:** Light resistance bands
**Week 5+:** Gradual weight increase IF pain-free

## Red Flags (Stop Immediately)

- Numbness/tingling in legs/feet

- Any symptom resembling 2019 episode
- Foot pain from impact/compression
